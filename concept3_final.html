SAP MM Fiori Self-Initiated Training Case

An end-to-end SAP procurement transformation, designed and modeled solo

I built this as a self-driven simulated SAP case study to apply my SAP Technology Consultant training to a realistic problem: a procurement process built on email requests, 1–3 day manual approvals, and Excel/Word-based PO creation that carried a 30% error rate in 3-way matching.

I redesigned the process around SAP Fiori — automated budget checks, threshold-based approval routing, and system-generated POs — and modeled the resulting impact end-to-end, including a full ROI breakdown and a phase-2 roadmap for barcode goods receipt and inventory integration.
